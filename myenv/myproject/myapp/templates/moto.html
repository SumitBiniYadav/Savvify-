FAQ
We've got the answers

Which apps does Savvify support? +
Savvify currently supports top food and grocery delivery apps in India, including Swiggy, Zomato, Blinkit, Zepto, and BigBasket.
How does Savvify track my spending? +
Savvify analyzes your order confirmation emails or connected transaction data to track your spending automatically across delivery apps.
Can I set a monthly budget? +
Yes, you can easily set a custom monthly spending limit. Savvify will alert you as you approach or exceed your budget.
Will I get notified if I overspend? +
Absolutely. Savvify sends smart alerts when you’re nearing or crossing your budget to help you stay in control of your spending.
Is my data safe with Savvify? +
Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All data is encrypted, and we never share or sell your personal information.
Can I track spending app-wise? +
Yes, Savvify breaks down your spending per app, so you can clearly see where your money is going each month.
