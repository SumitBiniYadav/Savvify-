Frequently Asked

Common Questions

How does Savvify track my spending?

Savvify securely connects to your delivery app accounts and analyzes your order history to provide detailed spending insights and analytics.

Is my data safe with Savvify?

Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption and never store your login credentials. Your data is completely secure and private.

Which apps does Savvify support?

We support major food delivery apps like Zomato, Swiggy, Uber Eats, and grocery apps like Instamart, Zepto, Amazon Fresh, and more.

Can I set spending limits?

Yes! You can set daily, weekly, or monthly spending limits and receive alerts when you're approaching or exceeding them.

How much does Savvify cost?

Savvify offers a free tier with basic features, and premium plans starting at ₹99/month for advanced analytics and unlimited tracking.

Can I get refunds for orders?

Savvify helps you track and manage spending, but refunds must be processed through the original delivery app. We can help you identify patterns to avoid unwanted orders.
